## Overview
**Oxaam** provides access to shared premium accounts for various popular services. It bypasses paywalls by sharing session cookies or credentials.

> [!CAUTION]
> **Use at your own risk.** These are shared accounts. Do not store sensitive data. This service resides in a legal gray area.

## Supported Services
- **Kaanvah Pro** (Canva Pro)
- **ChatGPT Premium**
- **Crunchyroll Premium**
- **Perplexity Max**
- **Scribd Premium**
- **Skillshare Premium**
- **TradingView Premium**

*(Note: Names are often intentionally misspelled on the site to avoid detection)*

## How to Access
1.  **Login**: The site asks for login credentials, but **NO authentication is enforced**. You can enter **ANY** random email, password, or mobile number to get in.
2.  **Canva Pro**: The OTP login method is currently broken. Use the **Cookie-Editor method** provided on the page to access Canva Pro.

> [!WARNING]
> **Privacy Alert**: Since these are shared accounts, your edits (e.g., in Canva) are visible to other users.
> **Pro Tip**: Always **delete your data** and designs after downloading them. Do not leave personal work in these accounts.
